Origin

Lionel Messi was born, 24 June 1987, in Rosario, Argentina to a working-class family. His father was a factory steel worker, and his mother a cleaner. He began playing from an early age. He played in the streets of Argentina until he was 11 years old where him and his father moved to Spain for his chance to play in the best club in Spain. This club was Barcelona. He played for the youth team and was eventually brought up to play for the actual team where he still plays to this day.

Achievements

Messi has had many achievements throughout his career so far and is still reaching for the stars. He has just recently won the Ballon d'Or 2019. This makes it his 5th in his career. He has scored 575 times out of 681 game appearances. Although everyone judges him for his international play because he has yet to win a major tournament for Argentina but he has done a lot for his club team Barcelona. He has spent his entire professional career with Barcelona, where he has won a club-record 34 trophies, including 10 La Liga titles, 4 UEFA Champions League titles and 6 Copas del Rey.
